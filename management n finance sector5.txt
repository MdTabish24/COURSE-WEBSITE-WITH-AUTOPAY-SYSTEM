
1. Digital Marketing Specialist
•	Objective: To master online marketing strategies across platforms, analytics, and campaign optimization.
•	Duration: 3-4 Months
Module 1: Digital Marketing Fundamentals
•	Evolution of digital marketing vs. traditional.
•	Digital ecosystem: Channels (Social, Search, Email, Content).
•	Consumer journey: Awareness, Consideration, Conversion, Retention.
•	Legal aspects: GDPR, Data privacy, Copyright.
Module 2: Website & SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
•	Website basics: CMS (WordPress), Domain/Hosting setup.
•	On-Page SEO: Keywords, Meta tags, URL structure, Content optimization.
•	Off-Page SEO: Backlinks, Guest posting.
•	Technical SEO: Site speed, Mobile-friendliness, XML Sitemaps.
•	Tools: Google Analytics, Google Search Console, SEMrush (free tier).
Module 3: Content & Social Media Marketing
•	Content strategy: Blogging, Video, Infographics.
•	Social platforms: Facebook/Instagram Ads Manager, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube.
•	Content calendar, Hashtag research, Engagement tactics.
•	Influencer marketing basics.
Module 4: Paid Advertising (PPC & Display)
•	Google Ads: Search, Display, Shopping campaigns.
•	Keyword research (Google Keyword Planner), Bidding strategies (CPC, CPM).
•	Facebook/Instagram Ads: Targeting, A/B testing.
•	Retargeting and Remarketing.
Module 5: Email & Affiliate Marketing
•	Email platforms (Mailchimp), List building, Automation sequences.
•	Affiliate programs (Amazon Associates), Commission tracking.
•	Conversion optimization (Landing pages, CTAs).
Module 6: Analytics & Strategy
•	Google Analytics: Traffic sources, Bounce rate, Conversion tracking.
•	ROI calculation, Campaign performance metrics (CTR, ROAS).
•	Digital marketing plan development.
Project: Create a full digital marketing campaign for a local business (SEO audit + social ads + analytics report).

2. Tally Prime & GST Professional
•	Objective: Hands-on training in accounting software with GST compliance for businesses.
•	Duration: 2-3 Months
Module 1: Tally Prime Fundamentals
•	Installation and Company Creation.
•	Masters: Ledgers, Groups, Stock Items, Units of Measure.
•	Currencies and Multi-currency transactions.
Module 2: Inventory Management
•	Stock Groups, Stock Categories, Godowns.
•	Purchase & Sales Vouchers: Order, Invoice, Receipt.
•	Batch-wise details, Expiry tracking.
•	Stock Valuation methods (FIFO, LIFO, Average).
Module 3: Accounting Features
•	Voucher Entry: Payment, Receipt, Contra, Journal.
•	Cost Centers and Profit Centers.
•	Bank Reconciliation, Cheque printing.
•	Trial Balance, Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet.
Module 4: GST in Tally
•	GST Registration and Configuration (Regular vs. Composition).
•	GST Ledgers (CGST, SGST, IGST).
•	GST Invoices: Taxable, Exempt, Export.
•	Input Tax Credit (ITC), Reverse Charge Mechanism.
•	Returns: GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-9 (Annual).
Module 5: Advanced Features & Reports
•	Payroll in Tally: Employee creation, Salary processing, TDS.
•	MIS Reports: Cash Flow, Ratio Analysis.
•	Data Security, Backup/Restore.
•	E-Way Bill Generation.
Project: Set up Tally for a retail business, record 1-month transactions, file GST returns, and generate financial reports.

3. Business Accounting & Taxation (BAT)
•	Objective: Comprehensive understanding of financial accounting, auditing, and Indian taxation laws.
•	Duration: 4-6 Months
Module 1: Financial Accounting Principles
•	Accounting Concepts: Double Entry System, GAAP, IFRS basics.
•	Journal, Ledger, Subsidiary Books (Cash Book, Purchase/Sales Book).
•	Depreciation methods (SLM, WDV), Rectification of Errors.
Module 2: Final Accounts
•	Trading Account, Profit & Loss Account, Balance Sheet.
•	Adjustments: Outstanding Expenses, Prepaid Income, Bad Debts.
•	Partnership Accounts: Admission, Retirement, Dissolution.
Module 3: Corporate Accounting
•	Company Accounts: Share Capital, Debentures, Forfeiture.
•	Amalgamation, Absorption, Liquidation.
•	Cash Flow Statement (Direct/Indirect Method).
Module 4: Income Tax
•	Heads of Income: Salary, House Property, Business/Profession, Capital Gains, Other Sources.
•	Deductions (80C to 80U), Tax Slabs (Old vs. New Regime).
•	TDS, Advance Tax, ITR Filing (Forms 16, 26AS).
Module 5: Indirect Taxes & Compliance
•	GST Framework: Supply, Input Tax Credit, Composition Scheme.
•	Customs Duty, Excise (Basics).
•	Audit: Internal Audit, Tax Audit (Section 44AB).
•	MSME Compliance.
Project: Prepare complete books of accounts for a small business, compute taxes, and file mock returns.
