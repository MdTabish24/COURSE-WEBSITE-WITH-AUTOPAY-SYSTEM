About Universal Skills
Universal SkillTech University, widely known as Universal Skills, is an ISO 9001:2015 Certified Skill Development Training Academy committed to empowering youth with industry-relevant skills and career opportunities. Our mission is to bridge the gap between education and employment by providing practical, job-oriented training that prepares students for the real demands of the modern workforce.
Over the years, Universal Skills has successfully trained more than 12,000 students across multiple sectors, and over 3,000 candidates have secured successful placements through our dedicated career support and industry partnerships. These achievements reflect our commitment to delivering quality skill education and real employment opportunities.
Our academy is supported by a team of highly trained and experienced expert trainers who bring real industry knowledge into the classroom. Training is delivered through modern teaching methodologies, practical workshops, and the latest equipment, ensuring that students gain hands-on experience and practical skills rather than just theoretical knowledge.
Universal Skills offers short-term and job-oriented programs ranging from 3 months to 1 year, covering a variety of high-demand sectors such as Information Technology, Beauty & Wellness, Retail & Sales, Healthcare, Hospitality, Technical Trades, and Entrepreneurship. Some of the popular courses offered include Digital Marketing, Tally with GST, Web Designing, Beauty Therapist, Retail Sales Associate, CCTV Technician, and Entrepreneurship Development, among others.
Beyond training programs, Universal Skills actively collaborates on Government Skill Development Initiatives, Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) projects, and International Skill Development programs, working with organizations to expand access to quality training and employment opportunities for youth.
At Universal Skills, our goal is not only to provide certifications but to build real careers and sustainable livelihoods. By combining experienced trainers, modern infrastructure, industry-aligned curriculum, and strong placement support, we continue to create a platform where students can develop practical skills, gain confidence, and move towards a successful professional future. 🚀
